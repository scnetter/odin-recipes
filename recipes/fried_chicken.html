Fried Chicken

Description

You can't beat the old standards! Who doesn't love some good, old Southern Fried Chicken?

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken - cut up
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons onion powder
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • peanut oil for frying

Steps

  1. Place the cut up chicken in a bowl. Sprinkle the salt, garlic and onion powder on top to cover.
  2. Add 2 cups buttermilk and stir until combined. Can cover in fridge to marinate for at least 2 hours.
  3. In a medium sized bowl, whisk together 2 cups all-purpose flour, 1 cup cornstarch, 2 teaspoons kosher salt, 2 teaspoons paprika, and 2 teaspoons black pepper - set aside.
  4. Take a piece of chicken at a time, let excess buttermilk drip off, and coat in the flout mixture. Set aside until coating looks pasty.
  5. Carefully add chicken to deep fryer (don't add more that 3-4 pieces at a time. Turn every few minutes until golden brown. When internal temperatures are 165, the chicken is done. Average time should be 8 - 14 mins with dark meat being 12 -14, white meat being 8 - 10.
  6. Remove chicken and place on a paper towel lined sheet tray.
  7. Serve.
